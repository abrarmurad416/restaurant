About Us

Experience the finest Indian and Pakistani cuisine at Khan Lahori Chaska - located in Westwood Square.

Step into Khan Lahori Chaska and be transported to the vibrant streets of Lahore and bustling markets of Delhi. Experience the lively ambiance as you savor our diverse selection of traditional Indian and Pakistani dishes, showcasing the rich culinary heritage of the region.

Indulge in our specialties, including biryani, mutton and chicken karai, parantha, and delightful weekend offerings of special paya and nihari. Immerse yourself in our community's warm hospitality and friendly atmosphere. Don't miss the chance to start your day with our delectable channa Puri or paranthe for a satisfying breakfast.

Visit Khan Lahori Chaska today and embark on a culinary journey through the vibrant flavors of Indian and Pakistani cuisine. We look forward to serving you and creating an unforgettable dining experience.
